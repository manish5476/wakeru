## 1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our Service to you.

### 1.1. Information You Provide to Us

*   **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect personal details such as your **first and last name, email address, phone number, and a secure password**. You may also choose to add a **profile picture**.
*   **Financial Information:** To use our expense tracking features, you may provide information related to your **expenses, income, budgets, and financial goals**. This includes transaction amounts, dates, categories, and descriptions.
*   **Receipt and Media Uploads:** If you use our receipt scanning feature, we collect the images you upload. This may include details from the receipts themselves. Our Optical Character Recognition (OCR) feature processes these images to extract transaction data.
*   **Social and Travel Information:** Our "friends" and "travel plan" features allow you to connect with other users and plan trips. We collect information about your **connections, shared expenses, and travel itineraries**.
*   **Feedback and Communication:** If you contact us for support, provide feedback, or participate in surveys, we will collect the information you include in your communications.

### 1.2. Information We Collect Automatically

*   **Usage Data:** We automatically collect information about how you interact with our Service. This includes the features you use, the actions you take, and the time, frequency, and duration of your activities.
*   **Device Information:** We collect information about the device you use to access our Service, including the **hardware model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information**.
*   **Log Data:** Our servers automatically record information ("Log Data") created by your use of the Service. This Log Data may include your **IP address, browser type, the pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and other statistics**.
*   **Location Information:** With your consent, we may collect information about your precise location to enable location-based features. You can enable or disable location services through your device settings.

### 1.3. Information from Third-Party Services

*   **Third-Party Authentication:** You can create an account using third-party services like **Google and Apple**. If you do, we will receive personal information from that service, such as your **name, email address, and profile picture**, as permitted by your profile settings on that service.
*   **External Services:** We use third-party services for functionalities like exchange rate lookups. While you are not directly providing information to them, your use of these features may involve data transfer to these services.
